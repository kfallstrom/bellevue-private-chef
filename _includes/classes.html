Client Appreciation Gatherings

Every couple months, I love to invite clients over for entertainment, connection, food and wine, knowledge, and fun. Held in lovely spaces including my herb and tea garden (weather permitting), the only requirement is to come prepared to relax and enjoy and donate a bottle of wine to appreciate together. Clients will also be invited over to join themed photo shoots and take home selections from my summer garden's bounty.

By email invitation only, first come, first serve!
