Giving Back

The Joy of Generosity

As much as the generous people and community of the Greater Seattle Area have blessed me, I enjoy giving back to them. I have served as volunteer executive chef to several outreach programs, helping the underserved such as homeless, mentally ill and people struggling with food and substance addictions. Seattle Union Gospel Mission, Montana Meth Project, Goodwill, Jubilee Reach, Congregations for the Homeless and Cambodian University Scholarship Program which enables bright, poor, rural Cambodian students to attend and graduate high school, vocational and universtity programs in Cambodia will receive a minimum of 5% proceeds.
